Authenticator Plus

Better way to manage 2-step enabled accounts



What is Authenticator Plus?

Authenticator Plus generates 2-step verification codes which will protect your accounts with both your password and your phone / tablet. With Authenticator Plus you can seamlessly sync and manage all your 2-step enabled accounts in phones / tablet / kindle.



Secure

Your account details are protected with 256-bit AES encryption, key is derived from your password using PBKDF2 with 64000 iterations. More info

Sync

Account details are seamlessly synced between devices (Phone, Tablet, Kindle).

Customization

Customize to your need, enable PIN lock, re-oder, theme and easy navigation.




Encrypted data

Your account details are protected with 256-bit AES encryption, key is derived from your password using PBKDF2 with 64000 iterations. So even if your device is rooted your data is still protected. More info


Sync across devices

Account details are seamlessly synced between devices (Phone, Tablet and Kindle). Accounts can be edited offline, when device goes online, data will be synced autotmatically.


Backup / Restore

Backup / Restore all account details even if you are are using non-rooted device, so no worries if you are migrating to a new device.


Import from Google Authenticator

ROOT ONLY: Accounts can imported from Google Authenticaor with free import plugin [https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mufri.authenticatorplus.legacyimport]
